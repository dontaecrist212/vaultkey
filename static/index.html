// HOW YOUR DATA IS PROTECTED

🔐 PASSWORD ENCRYPTION
All passwords are encrypted using AES-256 Fernet encryption before being stored. Even direct database access would only reveal encrypted data.
🛡️ ACCOUNT SECURITY
Your master password is hashed with SHA-256 and a unique salt — never stored in plain text. After 5 failed attempts your account locks for 15 minutes.
📱 TWO FACTOR AUTHENTICATION
Optional MFA using Google Authenticator or Authy. Even with your password, attackers cannot access your vault without your phone.
⏱️ AUTO LOCK
Your vault automatically locks after 5 minutes of inactivity.
🔒 SECURE CONNECTION
All data transmitted over HTTPS. Stored in a secure PostgreSQL database with isolated user accounts.
🔴 BREACH DETECTION
Passwords checked against HaveIBeenPwned using k-anonymity. Only 5 chars of the SHA-1 hash are sent — your password never leaves your device.
